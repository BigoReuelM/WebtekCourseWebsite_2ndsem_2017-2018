Internet

The Father of the Internet

Vint Cerf an american Internet pioneer.One of the inventors of the internet.

Internet is established during the year 1969 is the global system of interconnected computer networks that use the standard Internet Protocol Suite (TCP/IP) to serve billions of users worldwide. the internet carries information resources and services, like the electronic mail, application of world wide web, inter-liked hypertext document and many more. Internet is a tool use to create a network that would link computer to another computer to serve billions of users worldwide. It is a networks that consists of millions of private, public, academic, business, and government networks of local to global scope, that are linked by a broad array of electronic, wireless, and optical networking technologies.

ARPANET – (Advanced Research Projects Agency Network) an early packet switching network and the first network to implement the protocol suite TCP/IP.(foundation of the internet)

DARPA – (Defense Advanced Research Projects Agency) research program to allow networked computers to communicate transparently across multiple, linked packet networks.
